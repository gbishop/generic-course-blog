Welcome to Comp 116! I know many of you expected Professor McMillan to be your teacher this semester. Unfortunately, he has been seriously injured and I am here to take his place. He is expected to recover but won't likely be back to teaching until the Fall.

In this course you will learn that scientists use computers to collect, analyze, and display data; and how to do it yourself. My goal is for you to leave the course with tools that will help you in whatever scientific domains you pursue.
